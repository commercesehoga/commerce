FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are SP Notes chapter-wise or subject-wise?
SP Notes are subject-wise — one full PDF per subject (Microeconomics, Macroeconomics, Indian Economy). PPT Slides, Digital Notes, Handwritten Notes, and Mind Maps are available chapter-wise.
Are these notes completely free?
Yes. All notes on ThunderStudy are 100% free — no registration, no payment, no hidden charges.
What is the difference between Topper and Backbencher Notes?
Topper Notes are detailed, concept-heavy subject-wise PDFs for thorough preparation. Backbencher Notes are ultra-compact point-form subject-wise PDFs — perfect for last-minute revision the night before your exam.
Are these notes valid for CUET?
Yes. The CUET section has notes aligned to the NTA CUET Economics syllabus covering all three parts — Micro, Macro and Indian Economy.
Which formats are available and are they all chapter-wise?
SP Notes — subject-wise PDF (one per subject). PPT Slides, Digital Notes, Handwritten Notes, Mind Maps — all chapter-wise. Special Notes (Topper/Backbencher) — subject-wise PDFs (3 per course).
Which classes are covered?
CBSE Class 11 (Micro & Macro — Digital Notes only), Class 12 (Micro, Macro & Indian Economy — all 5 formats), and NTA CUET Economics (Micro, Macro & Indian Economy — all 5 formats).
How often are notes updated?
Notes are reviewed and updated before each academic year to reflect any CBSE or NTA syllabus changes.
