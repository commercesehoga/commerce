FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are SP Notes chapter-wise or subject-wise?
SP Notes are subject-wise — one full PDF per part (Part I: Accounting for Partnership Firms & Companies, Part II: Analysis of Financial Statements). PPT Slides, Digital Notes, Handwritten Notes, and Mind Maps are available chapter-wise.
Are these notes completely free?
Yes. All notes on ThunderStudy are 100% free — no registration, no payment, no hidden charges.
What is the difference between Topper and Backbencher Notes?
Topper Notes are detailed, concept-heavy subject-wise PDFs for thorough preparation. Backbencher Notes are ultra-compact point-form subject-wise PDFs — perfect for last-minute revision the night before your exam.
Are these notes valid for CUET?
Yes. The CUET section has notes aligned to the NTA CUET Accountancy syllabus covering all chapters from both parts.
Which formats are available and are they all chapter-wise?
SP Notes — subject-wise PDF (one per part). PPT Slides, Digital Notes, Handwritten Notes, Mind Maps — all chapter-wise. Special Notes (Topper/Backbencher) — subject-wise PDFs (2 per course).
Which classes are covered?
CBSE Class 11 (Financial Accounting Part I & II — Digital Notes only), Class 12 (Part I & II — all 5 formats), and NTA CUET Accountancy (Part I & II — all 5 formats).
How often are notes updated?
Notes are reviewed and updated before each academic year to reflect any CBSE or NTA syllabus changes.
